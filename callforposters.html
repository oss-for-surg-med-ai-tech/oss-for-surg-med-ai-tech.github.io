Poster Guidelines

Poster Size:
A0 Portrait (841 mm wide Ã— 1189 mm tall)

Sections to Include:

  • Title
  • Authors & Affiliations
  • Introduction / Background
  • Methods / Approach
  • Results / Findings
  • Conclusion / Future Work
  • References (if applicable)
  • Contact Information / QR Code (optional)

Design Tips:

  • Use large, legible fonts (minimum 24 pt for body text).
  • Include high-resolution images and figures.
  • Keep text concise and visually balanced with graphics.
  • Use bullet points where possible for clarity.

Submission Format:
PDF format for initial review (final print-ready file required after acceptance).
