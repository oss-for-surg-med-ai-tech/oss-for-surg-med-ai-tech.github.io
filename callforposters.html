Poster Submission

Abstract Submission Guidelines

Submission Overview:
Submit a 2-page abstract for review. Accepted abstracts will be invited for poster presentation.

Sections to Include:

  • Introduction
  • Materials and Methods
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • References

Formatting Requirements:

  • Length: Maximum of 2 pages (DIN A4 size: 210 mm × 297 mm)
  • Margins: 25 mm (top/bottom), 20 mm (left/right)
  • Layout: Two columns, each 80 mm wide, with 10 mm spacing between columns
  • Fonts:
    • Title: 14 pt, bold
    • Authors: 14 pt, normal
    • Affiliations & Email: 12 pt, italics
    • Section Headings: 10 pt, CAPITALS, bold
    • Main Text: 10 pt, normal
    • Figure Captions & References: 9 pt, normal

Templates:
Please use the Hamlyn abstract submission template.

Submission Format:
Submit as a PDF via OpenReview using the link above.
